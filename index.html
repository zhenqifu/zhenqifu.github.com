About Me

I am currently an tenure-track Associate Professor at the Hangzhou Institute of Technology / School of Artificial Intelligence, Xidian University (XDU). Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University (THU), working with Prof. Qionghai Dai (Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering) at the BBNC Lab. I received my Ph.D. degree in Signal and Information Processing from Xiamen University (XMU), China, in Dec. 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Xinghao Ding at the SmartDSP Lab. From Dec. 2021 to Dec. 2022, I was a visiting Ph.D. student at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, advised by Prof. Kai-Kuang Ma (IEEE Fellow). My current research interests include low-level vision, biomedical imaging, fluorescence microscopy imaging, and deep learning.
