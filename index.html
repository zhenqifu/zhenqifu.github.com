About Me

  • I am a postdoc researcher in the department of automation, Tsinghua University (THU), working with Prof. Qionghai Dai (Fellow of the Academy of Engineerning, China). Prior to this, I received the Ph.D. degree in signal and information processing from Xiamen University (XMU), China, in Dec. 2023, advised by Prof. Xinghao Ding at SmartDSP Lab. I was a visiting Ph.D. student with Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, from Dec. 2021 to Dec. 2022, advised by Prof. Kai-Kuang Ma (IEEE Fellow). My current research interests include low-level vision, biomedical imaging, and deep learning.
