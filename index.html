About Me

I am a postdoc researcher in the department of automation, Tsinghua University (THU), working with Prof. Qionghai Dai at BBNC Lab. Prior to this, I received the Ph.D. degree in signal and information processing from Xiamen University (XMU) in Dec. 2023. My research interests include low-level vision, biomedical imaging, fluorescence microscopy imaging, and deep learning.
